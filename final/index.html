Who We Are

At Guerrilla Fitness, our main objective is to satisfy. We offer a wide selection of stations and activities to help you grow in the way that you want to grow. This selection includes a large array of free weights for lifters of all fitness levels, cardio machines, yoga classes, an outdoor and indoor track, and our signature Guerrilla training room. We also have a carefully selected staff of trainers who will guide you to get the results you were thinking about when you made that New Year's resolution.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an entertaining, educational, friendly & inviting, and unique experience of unmatched quality that meets the health and fitness needs of everyone who has the desire to better themselves.
